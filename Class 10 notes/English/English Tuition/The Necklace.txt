This story was written by Guy De Maupassant (Gee da mupasa)
Full name: Henri Rene Albert 

He was born on 5 August 1850
And he died on 6 July 1893 

He was a 19th century french author, remembered as the master of short story and as a representative of the Naturalist School 
who described human lives and destinies and social forces in disillutioned and often pessimistic terms
