Age of Industrialisation: the dramatic changes which were seen in the society during in the past which brought a lot of changes to the industrial system is called age 
                          Ago of Industrialisation. 
                        
Types of Industrialisation: 
    (i) Agro based: agriculture; cotton textile, jute, etc. 
    (ii) Mineral based: mineral and resource based; iron, steel, etc. 

Benefits of industrialized: 
    (i) More money, i.e., more national and per capita income. 
    (ii) Develops rural and urban areas. 

Industries were mostly set up in the colonies because they had a lot of abundant resources. 

There were 13 American colonies and they imported lumber, rice, tobacco, iron, etc. to Britain. 

Indian silk and cotton dominated the ancient market.
