When two or more substances combine with each other and form a new substance/matter is called chemical reaction. 

Properties of chemical reaction: 
(i) Change in state 
(ii) Change in color 
(iii) Evolution of gases
(iv) Change in temperature 
(v) Formation of precipitate (deposition of insoluble particles)

Magnesium ribbon is cleaned before burning to avoid oxidation of the ashes and a smoky flame 

To get white dazzling flame or to remove the coating of carbonate, the magnesium ribbon is cleaned before buring (with rough material preferrable with sand paper)

Eg.- Magnesium + oxygen -> Magnesium Oxide
