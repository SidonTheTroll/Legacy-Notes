Sloth bears:
    Sloth bears live in the forested areas of India and the grasslands at the lower elevations
    Have shaggy hair and long mouth 
    Use their claws to dig 
    Their lips form a tube 
    Main food is termite 
    Their sucking sound can be heard from several feet away
